About Me

My father is a businessman, and my mother is also involved in business. I am passionate about technology and software development. Debugging and Validation

Issue: There was a missing alt attribute for one of the images, which could affect accessibility. How I Fixed It: I added a descriptive alt attribute to the image tag to ensure accessibility for visually impaired users. For example, I used alt="Kodjo". Result: The image is now accessible, and the validation tool reported no further issues with the image. .

I used the WAVE Accessibility Checker to check for accessibility issues. It found a few minor issues, including missing descriptive text for images (fixed with the alt attribute) and insufficient color contrast. How I Fixed It: I updated the contrast for some text elements to ensure sufficient readability. I also added more descriptive alt text for all images. Outcome: After these fixes, the site passed accessibility validation with no further issues.
